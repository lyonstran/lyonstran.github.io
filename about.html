About Me

Hi! I’m Lyons. I am a 3rd year university student passionate about data science and analytics. I particularly enjoy exploring ways to turn messy data into something meaningful. I enjoy working making projects based around statistics, programming, and making real-world applications.


A lot of my early projects were done for class projects and Datathons/Hackathons, and it was in these projects where I learned how to code and do statistical modeling, starting with Python, Java, and C++. These projects also helped me get familiar with various data science libraries like NumPy, Pandas, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, etc. As of recently, I've been learning R and it's plethora of data manipulation packages, SQL, Excel, and some basic shell scripting. In the somewhat near future, I hope to pick up Javascript, and to learn some frameworks like PyTorch and Django.
